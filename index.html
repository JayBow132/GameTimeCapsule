Gaming Highlights Through the Years

Here are some key achievements in gaming history:

1958

Tennis for Two

The first game designed purely for entertainment, Tennis for Two, was created by physicist William Higinbotham. It simulated a tennis match on an oscilloscope and is considered the origin of video gaming.

1980

Pac-Man

Namco’s Pac-Man became a cultural icon, introducing characters and gameplay that appealed to all ages. It remains one of the most recognizable and influential games of all time.

1996

Pokémon Red and Blue

These Game Boy classics launched the Pokémon franchise, captivating players with its "catch ‘em all" mechanic and sparking a global phenomenon.

2017

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

This critically acclaimed game redefined open-world gaming with its emphasis on exploration, creativity, and player freedom. It set a new standard for modern gaming experiences.
