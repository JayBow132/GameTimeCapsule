Influential Games That Changed Gaming

These are the games that shaped the gaming industry and left a lasting legacy on gaming culture.

List of Influential Games:

  • Super Mario Bros. (1985): Revolutionized the platformer genre and popularized side-scrolling games.
  • The Legend of Zelda (1986): Pioneered open-world exploration and puzzle-solving mechanics in action-adventure games.
  • Final Fantasy VII (1997): Brought cinematic storytelling and RPG elements to the mainstream.
  • Half-Life (1998): Defined the first-person shooter genre with a strong emphasis on narrative and AI-driven gameplay.
  • World of Warcraft (2004): Set the standard for massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs).
  • Fortnite (2017): Changed the battle royale genre and introduced innovative cross-platform play and in-game events.
