Iconic Games

2001: Halo: Combat Evolved

Gaming Milestone: Revolutionizing First-Person Shooters

Halo: Combat Evolved became a flagship title for the Xbox, setting new standards for first-person shooters with its engaging single-player campaign and multiplayer mode. It played a pivotal role in popularizing online gaming and solidified Halo as one of the most influential franchises in gaming history.

2004: World of Warcraft

Gaming Milestone: The MMORPG Explosion

World of Warcraft transformed the MMORPG genre, creating a vast, persistent online world with millions of players. Its massive success shaped the future of online gaming and became a cultural phenomenon, with its own unique community and lasting influence on game design.

2013: Grand Theft Auto V

Gaming Milestone: Pushing Boundaries in Open-World Gaming

Grand Theft Auto V set new standards in open-world game design, offering an enormous, detailed world with a gripping storyline and multiplayer options. Its combination of action, exploration, and storytelling made it one of the best-selling games of all time.
